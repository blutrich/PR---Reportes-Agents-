### Reporter Fit Warning

**This story is a marginal fit for Gad Lior.** CodeBuddy is a pure developer tool. No direct consumer angle, no cost-of-living connection, no bank/government failure. The brief stretches hard to find an economic angle (AI replacing jobs in tech), but this is a tech story dressed up in economic clothing. Gad Lior would likely see through the framing and pass. A tech reporter (e.g., Geektime, Calcalist Tech) is a more natural fit. The brief acknowledges this in the side note, which is honest but confirms the problem.

That said, the employment/AI angle is the best possible reframe. If a Gad Lior brief must be produced, the revised version below tightens the economic framing.
