## Client-Specific Banned Words
- "disrupting banking" / "משבשים את הבנקאות" — we complement, not disrupt
- "fintech revolution" / "מהפכת הפינטק" — too grandiose
- "empowering" / "מעצימים" — AI-coded and vague
- "democratizing finance" — buzzword, not our language
- "unique solution" / "פתרון ייחודי" — every startup says this
- "game-changer" — banned everywhere, especially here
